===========
Spreadsheet
===========

.. toctree::
   :titlesonly:

   spreadsheet/pivot

Spreadsheet let's you organize, analyze, and visualize your data in tabular form. Among others, you
can:

- Insert and link your Odoo data

  - :doc:`Pivot tables <spreadsheet/pivot>`
  - Charts
  - Lists
  - Menus

- Use default templates or create your own
- Format your data
- Use formulas and functions
- Sort and filter your data
